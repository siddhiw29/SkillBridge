## 🚀 **Getting Started**

1. **Open the Website**: Launch `index.html` in your browser
2. **Explore Subjects**: Click on subject cards to see tutors
3. **Find Tutors**: Use search and filters to find the perfect tutor
4. **Book Sessions**: Select dates and times for your sessions
5. **Track Progress**: Monitor your learning journey in the dashboard

## 🔗 **Navigation Flow**

```

    ↓
```
